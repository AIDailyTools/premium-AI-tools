Stopwatch

A simple and accurate stopwatch for timing events and activities.

How to Use

Our Stopwatch tool provides a precise way to measure time intervals for various activities. Whether you're timing workouts, cooking, presentations, or any other event, this digital stopwatch offers accuracy and ease of use.

Steps to use:

  1. Click the 'Start' button to begin timing.
  2. Use the 'Lap' button to record intermediate times without stopping the main timer.
  3. Click 'Stop' to pause the timer.
  4. Click 'Reset' to clear the timer and lap records.

Keep track of time with precision using our straightforward Stopwatch tool.
