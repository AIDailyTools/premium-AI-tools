Watermark PDF

Add custom text or image watermarks to your PDF documents for branding or security.

How to Use

Our Watermark PDF tool allows you to add custom text or image watermarks to your PDF documents. This is useful for branding, indicating document status (e.g., 'Draft', 'Confidential'), or protecting your content from unauthorized use.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload PDF' and select the document you wish to watermark.
  2. Choose to add a text watermark or an image watermark.
  3. Customize the watermark (e.g., text content, font, color, transparency, position, size for text; upload image, transparency, position, size for image).
  4. Preview the watermark on your document.
  5. Click 'Watermark PDF' and then 'Download PDF' to save your watermarked file.

Protect and brand your PDF documents with custom watermarks using our easy-to-use tool.
