GST Calculator

Calculate Goods and Services Tax (GST) for your transactions.

How to Use

Our GST Calculator helps you quickly add or remove Goods and Services Tax from any amount. This is essential for businesses and consumers in regions where GST is applicable, ensuring accurate pricing and invoicing.

Steps to use:

  1. Enter the original amount.
  2. Specify the GST rate (if different from default).
  3. Choose whether to add GST to the amount or remove GST from an inclusive amount.
  4. The calculated amount with or without GST will be displayed.
