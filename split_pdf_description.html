Split PDF

Divide a single PDF document into multiple smaller PDF files.

How to Use

Our Split PDF tool allows you to extract specific pages or ranges of pages from a large PDF document and save them as separate PDF files. This is useful for sharing only relevant sections of a document, reducing file size, or reorganizing content.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload PDF' and select the document you wish to split.
  2. Choose how you want to split the PDF: by page range, by every page, or by specific pages.
  3. Preview the selected pages to ensure accuracy.
  4. Click 'Split PDF' and then 'Download' your new individual PDF files.

Efficiently manage your PDF documents by splitting them into smaller, more manageable files.
