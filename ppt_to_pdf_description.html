PPT to PDF

Convert your Microsoft PowerPoint presentations (.ppt, .pptx) into easily shareable PDF files.

How to Use

Our PPT to PDF converter allows you to transform your PowerPoint presentations into a universal PDF format. This is perfect for sharing your slides without worrying about font compatibility, layout shifts, or the need for presentation software on the viewer's end.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload File' and select your PowerPoint presentation (.ppt or .pptx).
  2. The conversion process will start automatically.
  3. Once completed, click 'Download PDF' to save your new PDF file.
