Loan Calculator

Calculate loan payments, interest, and amortization schedules.

How to Use

The Loan Calculator helps you determine your monthly loan payments, total interest paid, and visualize the amortization schedule. This tool is useful for mortgages, car loans, personal loans, and more.

Steps to use:

  1. Enter the total loan amount.
  2. Input the annual interest rate.
  3. Specify the loan term in years.
  4. Click 'Calculate' to see your results.

The calculator will display your estimated monthly payment, the total amount you will pay over the life of the loan, and the total interest. You can also view a detailed amortization schedule.
