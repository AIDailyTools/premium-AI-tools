QR Code Generator Help

This tool creates custom QR codes for different types of data.

  • Select Tab: Choose the type of QR code you want to create (URL, Text, Email, SMS, PDF, Wi-Fi, or vCard).
  • Enter Data: Fill in the required fields for your selected tab.
  • Customize: Change the color of the QR code and optionally upload a logo to be placed in the center.
  • Generate: Click "Generate QR Code" to see the result.
  • Download: Right-click the generated QR code and select "Save image as..." to download it.
