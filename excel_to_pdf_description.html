Excel to PDF

Convert your Microsoft Excel spreadsheets (.xls, .xlsx) into professional PDF documents.

How to Use

Our Excel to PDF converter allows you to easily transform your spreadsheets into a static, shareable PDF format. This is ideal for preserving data integrity and ensuring that your financial reports, data tables, or charts appear exactly as intended, regardless of the viewer's software.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload File' and select your Excel spreadsheet (.xls or .xlsx).
  2. The conversion will begin automatically.
  3. Once complete, click 'Download PDF' to save your new PDF file.
