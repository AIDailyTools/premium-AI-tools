Calculate sales tax for purchases and transactions.

How to Use

Our Sales Tax Calculator helps you determine the exact sales tax amount for any purchase. This tool is invaluable for shoppers, businesses, and anyone needing to quickly calculate the total cost including sales tax, or to find the original price before tax.

Steps to use:

  1. Enter the original price of the item.
  2. Input the sales tax rate applicable to your location.
  3. Choose whether to add sales tax to the price or remove it from a total price.
  4. The tool will instantly display the sales tax amount and the final total.

Ensure accurate pricing and avoid surprises at checkout with our reliable Sales Tax Calculator.
