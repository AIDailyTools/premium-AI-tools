Calculate Value Added Tax (VAT) for your products and services.

How to Use

Our VAT Calculator simplifies the process of adding or removing Value Added Tax from any amount. This tool is crucial for businesses and individuals operating in regions with VAT, ensuring compliance and accurate financial planning.

Steps to use:

  1. Enter the net or gross amount.
  2. Specify the VAT rate applicable to your region.
  3. Choose whether to calculate VAT to be added or to extract VAT from a total amount.
  4. The tool will instantly display the VAT amount and the corresponding total/net amount.
