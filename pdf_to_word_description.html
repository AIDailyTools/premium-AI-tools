PDF to Word

Convert your PDF documents into editable Microsoft Word files (.doc, .docx).

How to Use

Our PDF to Word converter allows you to transform your PDF documents back into editable Word files. This is incredibly useful when you need to make changes to a document that you only have in PDF format, saving you time and effort from manual retyping.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload PDF' and select the PDF document you wish to convert.
  2. The conversion process will begin automatically.
  3. Once converted, click 'Download Word' to save your new editable Word file.
