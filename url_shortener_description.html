How to Use

Our URL Shortener transforms lengthy web addresses into short, manageable links. This is perfect for sharing on social media, in emails, or anywhere space is limited. Shortened URLs are easier to remember and type.

Steps to use:

  1. Paste your long URL into the input field.
  2. Click 'Shorten URL'.
  3. Copy the newly generated short URL.
  4. (Optional) Track clicks and other analytics for your shortened links.
