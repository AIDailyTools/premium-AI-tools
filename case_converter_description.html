Case Converter

Convert text between different cases like uppercase, lowercase, and title case.

How to Use

Our Case Converter tool allows you to quickly change the case of any text. Whether you need to convert to uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case, or even inverse case, this tool handles it all with ease.

Steps to use:

  1. Paste or type your text into the input area.
  2. Select the desired case conversion option (e.g., "UPPERCASE", "lowercase", "Title Case").
  3. The converted text will instantly appear in the output area.
  4. Copy the converted text for your use.

This tool is ideal for writers, developers, and anyone who needs to quickly reformat text case.
