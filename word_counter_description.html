Word Counter

Count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs in your text.

How to Use

Our Word Counter tool provides instant statistics on your text, including the number of words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs. It's an essential tool for writers, students, and professionals needing to meet specific word count requirements.

Steps to use:

  1. Paste your text into the provided text area.
  2. The word counter will automatically update with real-time statistics.
  3. Review the counts for words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.

No need to click any buttons; the analysis happens as you type or paste.
