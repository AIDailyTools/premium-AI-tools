Rotate PDF

Rotate specific pages or all pages in your PDF document to the correct orientation.

How to Use

Our Rotate PDF tool allows you to easily correct the orientation of pages within your PDF documents. Whether a page was scanned upside down or needs to be rotated for better viewing, this tool provides a simple solution to adjust individual pages or the entire document.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload PDF' and select the document you wish to rotate.
  2. Select the pages you want to rotate, or choose to rotate all pages.
  3. Choose the rotation angle (e.g., 90, 180, 270 degrees clockwise).
  4. Click 'Rotate PDF' and then 'Download PDF' to save your adjusted file.

Easily fix page orientations in your PDF documents for optimal viewing and printing.
