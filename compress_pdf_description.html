How to Use

Our Compress PDF tool helps you reduce the file size of your PDF documents, making them easier to share via email, upload to websites, or store on your devices. It intelligently optimizes images and other elements to achieve significant size reduction while maintaining document quality.

Steps to use:

  1. Click 'Upload PDF' and select the document you wish to compress.
  2. Choose your desired compression level (e.g., 'High Compression', 'Recommended', 'Less Compression').
  3. The tool will process and compress your PDF automatically.
  4. Once compressed, click 'Download PDF' to save your optimized file.
